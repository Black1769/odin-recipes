Veg Kolhapuri

Veg Kolhapuri is spicy and tasty mixed vegetable based dish has its origin in Kolhapur, a historical city in south Maharashtra.

Kolhapuri cuisine is known for its hot, spicy and pungent flavors.

Ingredients

  • Beans - 1 cup
  • Carrot - 1 cup
  • Capsicum - 1 cup
  • Panner - 1 cup
  • Cauliflower - 1 cup
  • Green Peas - 1 cup
  • Tomato â€“ 2
  • Cashew Nuts - 10 ( soaked for one hour)
  • Green Chilli - 4 to 5
  • Garam Masala - 1 tsp
  • Turmeric - 1 tsp
  • Red Chilli Powder - 1 tsp
  • Bay Leaf - 1 to 2
  • Cumin Seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Ginger - 1/2 tsp
  • Butter - 1 tbsp
  • Oil

Procedure

  1. Take a pan to fry all the vegetables, add oil to the pan and once its heated , add cauliflower and paneer, fry and keep it aside.
  2. Add beans, carrots and capsicum and fry. Keep all of the fried vegetables aside.
  3. To make the puree, add tomatoes to the mixie jar and make a fine paste.
  4. Now, add the soaked cashew nuts and make a fine paste.
  5. Take another pan, add oil and then add cumin seeds, bay leaves, turmeric, ginger paste, red chillies and saute them well.
  6. Now add garam masala, red chilli powder, water and saute them well.
  7. After five minutes, add the tomato and cashew paste and saute them.
  8. Now add the fried vegetables one by one.
  9. Add salt and butter and mix them well.
  10. Add water if necessary, so that the gravy becomes thick.
  11. Mix the entire gravy well.
  12. Add the chopped coriander leaves for garnishing.
  13. Delicious veg Kolhapuri is ready to serve.
